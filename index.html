Two sisters on a mission

We are a small business in the heart of karoo using herbs from our gardens and gardens in the area. We also infuse our ingredients to give delicious Flavour and nutritional value for your cooked meals

Local is lekkker

  • Locally Founded
  • Locally Growned
  • Locally Empowered

Watching our father plant and did gardening as a hobby has given our family a passion for gardening and health. We founded this business based on years of experiene and people constantly asking us, "Hoekom proe die kos soe lekker, wat is dit?". We love what we do. We wish and want to inspire our community that things are possible with hard work and sacrifice. We are deeply involved with the community. We love Calitzdorp

Products

We have a wide range of ingredients that is homegrown and locally sourced. Calitzdorp has a rich farming history and is known for its wine industry. It has a rich grape farming industry that are grown, crafted and used to make our local, international-renowed wine brands such as De Krans and Boplaas.

Calitzdorp has a wide range of farmers that grow different types of fruits and vegatables as well. Get the taste of rich resources that are used in our spices to make your food extra delicilous.
